Christmas Letter

Dear Dad,
I want to take this special moment to express my heartfelt gratitude for everything you’ve done for me. No words can truly capture how much I appreciate your love, care, and countless sacrifices. You’ve been my support, my motivator, and my greatest inspiration.

Thank you for being there through every high and low, for offering comfort when I needed it most, and for guiding me with your wisdom and patience. Even when I stumbled, your unwavering love always shone through. I see and cherish everything you do, and it means the world to me.

You've instilled in me the values of strength, perseverance, and integrity—lessons I carry with me every day. I’m incredibly fortunate to have you as my dad, and I hope you know how deeply loved and appreciated you are.

I promise to do my best to make you proud and to return even a small portion of the love and care you’ve given me. Thank you for being such an amazing father.

With all my love,
Zenji
